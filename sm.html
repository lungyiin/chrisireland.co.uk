Legal Innovation

A digital pocket guide

What is legal innovation?

A profession becoming comfortable with leading historical ways of working behind.

Involving new voices in the delivery of legal services, opening up the Ivory Tower of legal practice.

Why is it important?

Pressures on billing and evolving client expectations when it comes to the delivery of legal advice means that innovation is now vital for a law firm to survive.

Deregulation of the legal services market and the arrival of ALS providers means that clients have a wider choice for the best-value legal advice.

More than just technology

Technology isn't the only tool in the ability for a firm to innovate.

The technology used by law firms isn't particuarly innovative, but rather the application of the technolovy provides the innovation.

True innovation will include non-technological initiatives. This might include the use of legal design, legal project management.

Legal innovation is about changing mindsets

Lawyers becoming accepting of not being the only voice in the room that matters when it comes to legal.

The delivery of legal services through non traditional means.

A reinvention of the trusted advisor for the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

New voices

- Legal design experts
- Legal Project Managers (LPMs)
- Legal Technologists

Only one of these is a tech-focused role.
